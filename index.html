Paleo guide

Gluten-free, dairy free, and Paleo recipes to improve health.

Introduction

My quest for healthy eating came about two years ago. Our family faced with a new health crisis when our oldest daughter was diagnosed with a very rare connective tissue disorder.

Our whole family benefitted from healthy eating and soon discovered that when you eat healthy you feel healthy which not only improved my daughters condition but greatly improved our lives as well.

Learning the basics like ingredients, tools, and shopping tips will ease the transition into changing ones lifestyle of eating. These basic items are the foundation to using Paleo cooking to change or improve ones health

Ingredients

In order to cook Paleo it will be important to stock your pantry with the staples of Paleo cooking. When you have the right ingredients on hand, you will be more comfortable when making a Paleo recipe.

Flours

  • Almond Flour
  • Cashew Meal
  • Coconut Flour
  • Arrowroot Powder

Fats

  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Bacon Fat and Lard
  • Coconut Oil
  • Plam Shortening
  • Ghee

Milks

  • Almond Milk
  • Cashew Milk
  • Coconut Milk

Sweetners

  • Maple Syrup
  • Honey
  • Coconut Crystals

Tools to cook with

In order to be able to prepare whole raw foods in a resonable amount of time you will benefit from having some or all of these tools in your kitchen.

Pots and Pans

  • Made of heavy duty construction and non-toxic

Slow Cooker

  • Great for busy days

Blender

  • Creates smooth batters and doughs, puree soups, and make smoothies.

Food Processor

  • The work horse to chop or grate vegetables, nuts, seeds and fruits.

Spiralizer

  • Turns vegtables into noodles and meals become more exciting.

Sharp Knives

  • When your not eating all ready prepared and processed foods there will be more prep involved in meal preparation. You will need good variety and sharp knives at ready.

Parchment Paper

  • Never use toxic aluminium foil, switch to parchment paper to line pans.
